# Refund and Payment Policy

## 1. Fee Structure Overview

Course fees at HIAS are charged per term (Diploma courses), per semester (Bachelor and Master courses), or as listed on each course page. The published "total fee" covers tuition, standard learning materials and exam sittings. It does not cover: textbooks, uniform or equipment for clinical placements, re-sit examination fees, or graduation ceremony attendance (AUD 150 optional).

## 2. Payment Options

1. **Per-term / per-semester instalments** (default): invoiced 20 business days before each term starts; due by day 1 of teaching.
2. **Annual upfront payment**: Bachelor students paying a full year upfront receive a 5% discount on that year's tuition.
3. **Payment plan (fortnightly)**: students experiencing financial difficulty may apply for a fortnightly direct-debit plan through Student Services. An establishment fee of AUD 50 applies. Two consecutive missed direct debits revert the account to standard invoicing.
4. **Corporate sponsorship**: available for the MBA and Master of Project Management. The Institute invoices the employer directly on receipt of a signed sponsorship agreement.

Accepted methods: bank transfer, BPAY, Visa/Mastercard (1.2% surcharge). HIAS does not accept cash payments above AUD 500.

## 3. Refund Schedule

| Situation | Refund |
|---|---|
| HIAS cancels the course or intake | 100% of all monies paid, including deposit |
| Student cancels more than 20 business days before start | All fees paid refunded; deposit refunded less AUD 100 admin fee |
| Student cancels 20 business days or fewer before start | Fees refunded; AUD 500 deposit forfeited |
| Withdrawal within first 4 teaching weeks (before census) | Current term fees refunded at 80%; deposit forfeited |
| Withdrawal after census | No refund of current term fees; future terms not charged |
| Approved Special Circumstances withdrawal | 100% fee credit for 24 months, or 50% cash refund |
| Visa refusal (international applicants) before course start | 100% of tuition fees refunded less AUD 300 administration fee |

## 4. How Refunds Are Paid

- Refund applications are submitted through the Student Portal and processed within **15 business days** of approval.
- Refunds are paid to the same account or card used for the original payment.
- If a third party (parent, employer) paid the fees, the refund is returned to that third party.

## 5. Overdue Fees

- Invoices unpaid 5 business days after the due date incur a late fee of AUD 75.
- Accounts 20+ business days overdue are referred to the Enrollment Office and may result in enrollment cancellation under HIAS-POL-002.
- Students with outstanding fees cannot receive final results, transcripts, or testamurs until the balance is cleared.

## 6. Fee Increases

Fees are reviewed annually. Continuing students are protected by a fee-cap guarantee: tuition for a course already commenced will not increase by more than 3% per year. Published fees in the catalogue apply to the 2026-27 intakes.

## 7. Scholarships and Fee Waivers

HIAS offers a 20% Principal's Scholarship on Diploma courses for school leavers with an ATAR of 80+, and a 10% Alumni Discount on Master courses for HIAS Bachelor graduates. Scholarships apply to tuition only and cannot be combined.
